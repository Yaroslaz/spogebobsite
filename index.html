Squarepants

    The population of the underwater town of Bikini Bottom is made up of various marine life. Among them are SpongeBob's best friend, a starfish named Patrick, an irritable haughty squid named Squidward, and a squirrel named Sandy, who also lives underwater using a wetsuit. SpongeBob works as a chef at the Krusty Krab, run by old curmudgeon Mr. Krabs.

Facts :)

  1. To create SpongeBob's characteristic laugh, Tom Kenny hit his throat several times with his hand, saying, "Ahh."

  2. Spongebob lives at 124 Conch Street.

  3. Whenever there is chaos, the fish cries "My leg!"

4. Almost every time a character throws something, the glass breaks.

5. The Little Mermaid's stealth boat is a 1959 Cadillac.

6.Patrick was noticeably bigger in the first season. As the season progressed, he began to lose weight.

7.SpongeBob was originally called SpongeBoy, but that name had already been trademarked for the cleaner.
